About Us

PU's CodeSphere is a student-driven digital platform developed by the B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (CSE AIML) department. It serves as a centralized hub for showcasing student innovation, technical projects, and creative problem-solving initiatives across disciplines. CodeSphere is built with the vision of creating a structured ecosystem where ideas evolve into implementations and implementations become inspirations. The platform enables students to present their work, share GitHub repositories, and make their projects accessible to peers, faculty, and external audiences. As students specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, we believe that practical exposure is as important as theoretical knowledge. PU's CodeSphere reflects our commitment to applying modern technologies to build scalable, secure, and user-centric web applications.

Our Vision

To establish PU's CodeSphere as a dynamic digital space that promotes innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence while representing the technological strength of our institution.

Our Vision
